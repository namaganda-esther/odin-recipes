Roasted Chicken Recipe

Desciption

How to prepare roasted chicken in Uganda

Ingredients

  • Chicken – 6 pieces pre-roasted/ enkalilire
  • Onions – 2 big
  • Garlic – 4 cloves
  • Chicken gravy powder – 2 table Spoon
  • Chicken masala – 1 table Spoon
  • Cooking oil – 1 table Spoon
  • Cot mill – 1 spring
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method For Roasted Chicken

  1. Clean the chicken
  2. Clean and chop onions and garlic
  3. Crush the onions, garlic, salt, pepper, cot mill, chicken masala and chicken gravy
  4. Mix the crushed ingredients with oil then marinate the chicken pieces and keep it for between 10 – 15 minutes before roasting
  5. Place the chicken on a greased baking tray and put in a hot oven 180 ◦c
  6. Keep basting the chicken that is powering oil and extract chicken juices on the chicken while roasting
  7. Roast until golden brown and serve with a carbohydrate and a vegetable preferably raw vegetables.
